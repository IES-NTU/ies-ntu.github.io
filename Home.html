About Us & Our Work

The Institution of Engineers, Singapore was formally established on July 1966 as the national society of engineers in Singapore. IES is the premier engineering institution that is called upon by the government to take action regarding all engineering matters. IES NTU is a Student Chapter of the organization that consists of IES student members from NTU. IES NTU is a 2-tier club that is well represented amongst the students majoring in engineering at the undergraduate and graduate level. The club actively takes various initiatives and responsibilities to collaborate with various companies and provide enrichment to the engineering community at NTU. It takes upon itself to reach out to pre-university institutions and external organizations to organize workshops and events for students that impart great knowledge about engineering and technology. It encourages students to participate in different levels of competitions and provides a platform for the students to learn and grow. Till date, IES NTU has organized advanced python workshops, recruitment drives, and an array of industry visits to institutions. IES NTU has high aspirations for the progress and development of the club and its members and strives to bring better recognition to the engineering profession amongst all students.
